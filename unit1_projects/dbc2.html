About.




Well, I thought
I was born a captain
with a plan to fix the blue;
and, the only things missing:
a ship, the map, and crew.


The stars found me
stumbling into true north,
drunk off pirate meddling,
and steeped in raptured
vogue.


The world--
ever-churning sound,
stream of blue inseam.
The Sweet Song is found
sailing the middle-sea...
